## Routes and maps

- [Google Maps](https://maps.google.com/) for local directions and nearby places
- My Google My Maps for broad trip planning
- My Mapstr for personal notes on restaurants and hotels I have actually tried

## Translation

- Google Translate for text, speech and camera translation

## Luggage

- Bounce can help locate some luggage-storage options, but does not list every locker

## Booking tools

- Klook and GetYourGuide for comparing activities and selected transport products
- [Japan Experience](https://www.japan-experience.com/) for selected transport, accommodation, excursions and connectivity products

When a booking matters, compare the listing with the official operator or venue before paying.
