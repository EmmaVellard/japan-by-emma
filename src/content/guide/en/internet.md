## My preferred option

If your phone supports it, **an eSIM is often the simplest option**: arrange it before departure, keep your physical SIM in place and activate the data plan on arrival.

## Other options

Physical visitor SIMs and pocket Wi-Fi devices can still suit some groups or phones. Compare total data, hotspot rules, battery needs, collection and return points, and the full price.

**Public Wi-Fi should be a backup**, rather than your only connection, even though it is available in many stations, cafes and public areas.
