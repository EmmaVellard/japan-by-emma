## Look up—and downstairs

Restaurants are often **on upper floors or in basements**. The street-level shopfront is only part of what is available, so check building directories and map results nearby.

## How I read reviews

I do not rely on one score. I read recent comments and look at what people actually ordered before deciding whether a restaurant suits me.

## Queues are normal

**Queues are normal.** Popular restaurants often have lines, and not every place takes reservations. Keep one nearby alternative and avoid building an entire evening around a single unbookable restaurant.

## Konbini backup

Convenience stores are useful when it is late or you need something easy.
