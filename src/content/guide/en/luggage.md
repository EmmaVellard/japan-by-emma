## Coin lockers

Stations commonly have lockers in several sizes. Availability can be limited at busy times, and large suitcases need the biggest compartments. Save the station exit or locker area so you can find it again.

## Hotel storage

Ask whether your accommodation can hold bags before check-in or after check-out. Policies vary.

## Luggage forwarding

**Forwarding a large bag** can make multi-city travel much easier. Pack one night of essentials separately because delivery is not always same-day. Confirm cutoff times, excluded items and the receiving hotel’s details.
