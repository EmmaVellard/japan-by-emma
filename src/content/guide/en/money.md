## Carry more than one option

Cards are increasingly accepted, but **cash is still useful**—particularly at smaller restaurants, temples, rural businesses and older ticket machines. I carry some yen, a card without unnecessary foreign transaction fees, and **a backup card stored separately**.

## ATMs

Convenience-store and postal ATMs can be useful for international cards, but compatibility and fees depend on the card and operator. Tell your bank about the trip if required and keep its support number somewhere secure.
