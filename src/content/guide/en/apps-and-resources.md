## Everyday toolkit

- **Google Maps** for local routes and nearby places
- **Google Translate** for text, speech and camera translation
- **Bounce** for finding some luggage-storage locations
- **Klook** and **GetYourGuide** for comparing bookable activities and some transport products

## Before relying on an app

Download essential tickets, addresses and hotel details for offline access. App coverage, fees and listings change; always compare an important booking with the official operator or venue.
