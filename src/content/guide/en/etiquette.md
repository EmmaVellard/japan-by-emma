## In public spaces

- Avoid eating or drinking while walking; stop somewhere suitable instead.
- Keep your voice low and your belongings compact on public transport.
- Follow local signs and queues, even when the reason is not obvious.

## In restaurants

**Do not leave a tip.** It is not part of normal service culture, except in a few specific situations such as some private-guide services.

## A little Japanese goes a long way

In my experience, people were often kind when I looked lost, especially when I tried **a few words of Japanese**. Outside major cities, you may also attract a little curiosity. Stay patient and respectful.
