## Spring

**Cherry blossom season** is one of the busiest periods, but the scenery can be worth it. Forecasts are published each year and vary significantly by region, so check current local dates rather than planning around a fixed national week.

## Autumn

**Autumn foliage** can be just as spectacular and may feel calmer than peak blossom season. Colour arrives at different times from Hokkaido to Kyushu.

## Summer

I would personally avoid **July and August** if heat is difficult for you. High humidity can make the temperature feel much more intense, and popular places can be crowded.

## The useful rule

Japan changes dramatically by season. Choose the experience you want first, then check current forecasts for the exact regions on your route.
