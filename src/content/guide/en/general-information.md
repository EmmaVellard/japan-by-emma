## Konbinis are genuinely useful

Convenience stores such as **7-Eleven, FamilyMart and Lawson** are often open around the clock. Beyond snacks and meals, many have ATMs, printers and toilets. Services vary by branch, so treat them as a helpful option rather than a guarantee.

## Everyday safety

Japan felt **extremely safe to me**, including when travelling alone. Normal precautions still apply, especially with passports, money and late-night journeys.

## Carry a small rubbish bag

Public rubbish bins can be hard to find. You may carry packaging for a while, so keeping **a small reusable bag** in your day bag is surprisingly useful.

## Lockers

Coin lockers are common in stations and some shopping areas. The Bounce app can help you find luggage storage, although it does not list every option.
