## Start with flexible searches

I use **Google Flights early** and compare several departure days, airports and arrival cities. Osaka and Sapporo can be useful alternatives to Tokyo depending on the itinerary.

## Haneda or Narita

If I am landing in Tokyo, **I prefer Haneda** because it is generally easier to reach from the city centre. Compare the full journey, baggage conditions and arrival time—not only the headline fare.
