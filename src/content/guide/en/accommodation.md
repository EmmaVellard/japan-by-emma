## What to expect

Booking platforms list hotels, hostels, guesthouses, capsule hotels and **ryokan**, traditional Japanese inns. Some rooms use futons instead of beds; that is normal and can be more comfortable than expected.

## Luggage at hotels

Many hotels and hostels can **hold bags before check-in and after check-out**. Confirm directly with the property, especially for very small stays or unattended accommodation.

## Forwarding luggage

Japan has convenient luggage-delivery services between supported hotels and other counters. Reception staff can often help with the form. Delivery windows, accepted items and prices change, so confirm them for each transfer.
