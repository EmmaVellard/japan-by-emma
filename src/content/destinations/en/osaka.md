## My note

I like keeping Osaka flexible: it can be a lively day trip from Kyoto, a short stay of its own or the last chapter before flying home. It is a city I want to keep exploring, especially through its food.

## Planning role

Osaka can simplify the end of a Kansai itinerary if your flight leaves from the region. Decide the airport before booking a return to Tokyo.
