## The change of scenery I wanted

The feeling I wanted from this route was the transition from Tokyo to **Hokuriku and the Japanese Alps**. After a full week in the capital, Kanazawa, Takayama and Shirakawa-go make the second half feel smaller, calmer and more rooted in the landscape.

This route is less about adding more cities and more about contrast: broad Tokyo neighbourhoods first, then gardens, old streets, mountain roads and villages.

## Practical warning

Shirakawa-go and the mountain connections are more sensitive to bus schedules than major-city rail routes. **Verify the whole transfer chain** before reserving accommodation.
