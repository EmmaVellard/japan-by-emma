## The three-week story

This is my broadest first-trip route. Tokyo and Kyoto receive enough time to feel like real chapters rather than quick stops, then the journey begins to move: Nara, Osaka, Kobe, Kurashiki, Hiroshima, Miyajima and Himeji.

What I like about this route is the way it gradually changes scale. It begins with two cities that could fill entire trips, then moves towards **smaller streets, the Seto Inland Sea and places shaped by history**. The second half asks for more packing and unpacking, but it also gives the journey a strong sense of progression.

Choose the departure airport before fixing day 20. Returning to Tokyo may be unnecessary if the flight leaves from Osaka, and **Kobe or Himeji can become day trips** if you want fewer hotel changes.
